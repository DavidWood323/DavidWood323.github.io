Application for Freelance Proofreader

I would like to offer my application for proofreading services and if successful at the same time I would welcome supporting ESL students embarking upon their chosen career paths .

Both of my children were schooled abroad at International School and through the years I have recognised the efforts of quite a number of ESL students . My eldest child abroad entirely through to the end of A levels and the youngest until year 11 . From 1996 to 2014 I lived in Kota Kinabalu , East Malaysia . My children later attending Cardiff and Surrey Universities .

I do not have previous proofreading experience however my toolbox is equipped with fairly excellent English skills based on years of technical reporting .

My mainstream career was primarily ‘hands on’ however day to day operations were high cost and responsibility , thus a considerable level of technical and administrative reporting was a requisite on a daily and end of project basis . Reporting both to my employer and primarily to the client , Oil & Gas companies such as Shell , Chevron , PTTEP Thailand and many others .

Attention to detail was most likely the number one attribute required in my career .
